Classe com as equacoes da isoterma de Dubinin-\/\+Radushkevich 

Isoterma com dois parametros, $ q_{max} $ e $ K_1 $, cujas formulas sao as seguintes\+: \textbackslash{}begin\{align\} Q\+\_\+e (C\+\_\+e, T) \&= q\+\_\+\{max\} \textbackslash{}exp( -\/K\+\_\+1 \textbackslash{}epsilon$^\wedge$2 ) \textbackslash{}hfil \textbackslash{}\textbackslash{} \textbackslash{}\textbackslash{} \textbackslash{}epsilon \&= R T \textbackslash{}ln \textbackslash{}left(1 + \textbackslash{}frac\{1\}\{C\+\_\+e\} \textbackslash{}right) \textbackslash{}end\{align\} Um artigo de referencia pode ser encontrado \href{https://doi.org/10.1021/cr60204a006}{\texttt{ aqui}}. \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Autores}
